About us

Born on the
Grampians cliff face

Active Adventures started when two climbing guides decided Melbourne deserved better than tourist-trap day trips. We design routes and experiences that respect the land — and push the people on it.

Every guide on our team holds current Wilderness First Responder certification. We cap group sizes at 10 so every adventurer gets real attention. No filler, no fluff — just the outdoors, done properly.

  • ⛰
    Expert-led

    All guides are certified WFR and industry professionals.

  • 🌿
    Leave No Trace

    We operate under strict Leave No Trace principles on every route.

  • 🤝
    Small groups

    Maximum 10 per group. You're never just a number with us.
