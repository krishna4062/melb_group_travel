About us

Born on the
Grampians cliff face

Active Adventures started when two climbing guides decided Melbourne deserved better than tourist-trap day trips. We design routes and experiences that respect the land — and push the people on it.

Now operating across all eight states and territories, we bring the same no-nonsense approach to every corner of Australia — from the Red Centre to the Tasmanian wilderness, from the Great Barrier Reef to the Blue Mountains.

  • ⛰
    Expert-led

    All guides are certified WFR and industry professionals.

  • 🌿

  • 🤝
